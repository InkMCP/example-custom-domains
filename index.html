Custom Domains

Connect your own domain to your deployed app

How It Works

  1. Deploy your app with Ink
  2. Add your custom domain in settings
  3. Update your DNS records
  4. SSL is automatically provisioned

DNS Configuration

CNAME www your-app.ink.run
CNAME @ your-app.ink.run

Features

  • Automatic SSL/TLS certificates
  • Global CDN distribution
  • Zero-downtime domain switching
  • Support for apex and subdomains
